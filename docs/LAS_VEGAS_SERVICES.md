## Transportation

### Las Vegas Monorail
- **Operator:** Las Vegas Monorail Company
- **Stations:** 7 stations along the Strip
  - MGM Grand Station
  - Bally's/Paris Station
  - Flamingo/Caesars Station
  - Harrah's/The LINQ Station
  - Westgate Station
  - Convention Center Station
  - SAHARA Station
- **Features:** Wheelchair accessible, event shuttles
- **Role in Simulation:** Primary athlete transportation along Strip

### RTC of Southern Nevada
- **Operator:** Regional Transportation Commission
- **Routes:** 
  - Strip & Downtown Express (SDX)
  - Deuce on the Strip
  - Event Shuttle Routes
- **Features:** ADA compliant, real-time tracking
- **Role in Simulation:** Bus transportation for athletes

### Designated Ride-Share Zones
- **Operators:** Uber, Lyft
- **Locations:** Hotel pickup zones, venue drop-off zones, airport terminals
- **Features:** Accessible vehicles, event coordination
- **Role in Simulation:** Flexible transportation option

## Public Safety Agencies

### LVMPD (Las Vegas Metropolitan Police Department)
- **Role:** Public safety, traffic management, event security coordination
- **Area Commands:**
  - South Central Area Command
  - Convention Center Area
  - Strip Corridor
- **Services:** Community policing presence, traffic direction, event crowd management, emergency response coordination
- **Role in Simulation:** Primary public safety coordination

### Las Vegas Fire & Rescue
- **Role:** Fire suppression, emergency medical services, rescue operations
- **Stations:** Station 5 (Strip area), Station 18 (Convention Center area)
- **Services:** Medical first response, fire safety, emergency medical transport, event medical support
- **Role in Simulation:** Emergency medical response

### Clark County Fire Department
- **Role:** Fire and emergency medical services in Clark County
- **Services:** Emergency response, medical support, event coverage
- **Role in Simulation:** County-wide emergency services

### AMR Las Vegas (American Medical Response)
- **Role:** Emergency medical transportation
- **Services:** Ambulance transport, medical emergency response, hospital transport coordination
- **Role in Simulation:** Medical transport to hospitals
